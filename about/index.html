About Us

Neo Achasse

I'm a longtime on and off EVE player and a member of Karmafleet. I started Interstellar Systems because I hate myself and wanted to try the pain and suffering that is running industry in EVE. That did not seem to be enough, so I also decided to design and develop a website. I hope you find it useful.

What is ITS-S?

My vision for Interstellar Systems is to be a one-stop shop for industry related things. I aim to supply items from blueprints to ships, all in one place.

Current goal is to expand the store to include more items and eventually expand to offering more services like JF transport and buyback. Lets see how far i can get before burning out.

Contact

If you have questions, looking to skip the store, have some feedback or just need information, reach out to me directly. Eve-mail Neo Achasse (Karmafleet) or join the ITS-S Public channel.
